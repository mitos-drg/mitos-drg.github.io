Marble Tilt

Available on:

itch.io →

Marble Tilt is a simple, casual game inspired by classic marble maze games. Use your dexterity and manipulate your phone as the dots reacts to the real gravity. Complete all the levels belonging to various sections. Each section has its own features like colour-split obstacles, bouncing dots or walls. More new sections are coming soon!

To play Marble Tilt you need a device with accelerometer and touchscreen. For now the game only supports Android phones and tablets, but hopefully it would be ported to iOS and UWP in the near future.
